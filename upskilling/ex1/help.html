Community Event Portal Help Center

Welcome to the Local Community Event Portal support page. If you are having trouble registering for events, checking weekly schedules, or finding nearby coordinates, please read the instructions below:

  • Ensure Geolocation permission is granted when finding nearby events.
  • Use your local browser storage if you want your selected event preferences to persist.
  • Complete the entire registration form before navigating away, or you will see an alert page warning you of unsaved inputs.
